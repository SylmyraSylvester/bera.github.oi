CENDOL DURIAN KEMAYAN

Address: Kampung Baharu Sentosa, 28380 Kemayan, Pahang.

Cendol Durian Kemayan, located in Kemayan within the Bera district of Pahang, is renowned for its specialty cendol dishes. The highlight of the stall is the Cendol Durian, a delightful treat featuring rich and creamy durian flesh combined with icy cendol, creating a unique experience for durian lovers. In addition to this, they offer traditional cendol, which includes green jelly noodles, coconut milk, shaved ice, and palm sugar syrup (gula Melaka). The stall typically exudes a casual and relaxed atmosphere, making it an ideal spot to enjoy a refreshing treat on a hot day and savor the local flavors.

Cili Cheez

Address: Jalan Utama Bera, Sebertak, 28300 Triang, Pahang, Malaysia.

Cili Cheez in Bera, Pahang, is a well-loved eatery known for its unique fusion of spicy and cheesy dishes. The menu features a variety of items that combine bold flavors with a cheesy twist, appealing to those who enjoy innovative and flavorful meals. Popular dishes include cheesy noodles, spicy cheese fries, and their signature cheesy chicken. The casual and vibrant atmosphere makes it a great spot for dining with friends and family.

Restoran Tasek Bera Station

Address: 13, Jalan Kerayong 4, Bandar Baru Kerayong, 28200 Bandar Bera, Pahang

Menu Tradisi / Heritage offers a diverse range of traditional and Western foods. Highlights include Roti Bakar Arang, Bubur Nasi Ikan Haruan, Nasi Penyet Talapia, Sup Kambing Berempah, and Nasi Ayam Basmathi & Bakchoi. The Western menu features Chicken Chop, Lamb Chop, Spaghetti Carbonara, Fish n Chips, Meatballs, Burgers, and Roti John. In the afternoon, you can enjoy Rojak Ayam, Tahu Bakar, Tauho Sumbat, Young Tau Foo Kangkung, Roti Kukus, Kopi Kopitiam Kaw, Oden, and Yee Mee Hot Plate.

Nyiorr Kisoor

Address: No 3 gerai PLB, Kampung Mengkarak, Pahang

Nyiorr Kisoor in Bera, Pahang, is a popular spot known for its traditional Malaysian desserts and snacks. They specialize in a variety of coconut-based treats, including the famous "kisoor," a local delicacy made with coconut and other ingredients. The venue offers a cozy atmosphere where visitors can enjoy these authentic flavors in a relaxing setting.
